# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mastra users who want a local, installable Studio shell without having to assemble an Electron app themselves. They may be experienced developers, but the app should also work for users who are comfortable configuring local AI tools and lightweight code projects.

## Product Purpose

Mastra Studio Desktop opens bundled, local, development, and hosted Studio instances from one desktop surface. Success means users can quickly connect to a Mastra runtime, inspect or configure agents, and move between local and hosted Studios without managing browser tabs or Electron internals.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, developer-native.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, decorative dashboards, over-branded controls, browser chrome that fights the embedded Studio, and anything that makes the desktop shell feel heavier than the Studio it hosts.

## Design Principles

- Keep Studio as the product surface; the desktop shell should feel like lightweight chrome around it.
- Prefer familiar app-shell affordances over custom navigation inventions.
- Make local-first setup obvious, but keep hosted Platform access available when connected.
- Preserve density and speed for repeated developer workflows.
- Use restrained polish: clear hover, focus, loading, and disabled states without decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use system typography, keyboard-visible focus states, sufficient contrast for controls and labels, and reduced-motion fallbacks for all micro-interactions.
